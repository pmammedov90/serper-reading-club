Meet-up times for a Reading Club


Welcome to our Reading Club! Our reading club is a community of book enthusiasts who come together to explore the wonderful world of literature. During our reading times, we gather in a cozy and inviting environment where we share our love for books, engage in thoughtful discussions, and expand our knowledge through reading.


Our reading times are held on:


Mondays, for 2 hours at 10am at The London Library,
Wednesdays, for 2 hours at 12pm at The Regent's Park,
Fridays, for 2 hours at 10am at The Maughan Library,
Saturdays, for 2 hours at 12pm at Hyde Park,
Sundays, Reflect on books at 10am at Downhills Park Cafe


alongside with coffee, providing a regular opportunity for members to connect, exchange ideas, and discover new literary gems. Whether you're a seasoned bookworm or just starting your reading journey, our reading times offer a welcoming space to explore diverse genres, share recommendations, and foster a love for reading.
